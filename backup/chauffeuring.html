At first many of our competitors did not understand why Braendle Limousine offered this service. To be honest we also were not so sure, but with time we have experienced a great demand.

When life gets exciting, we don`t want you to miss out in comfort.

Your private driver is not available or you would like to work while having your own car with you?

Or perhaps you have got an invitation to a festivity out of town and would like to have a drink without disclaiming your own car?

Our certified Braendle Chauffeur will be at your disposal at any time and drive you to your desired appointments. We also would be pleased to provide a chauffeur even for a longer period, for example as a holiday replacement or in case of illness of your own chauffeur.
